ROLE PURPOSE

The aim is to state the overall significance of the job from the organization’s perspective. Responsible for the execution authority for large IT Solutions, with clear and extreme focus on solution delivery. Largely engaged in and accountable for the Delivery Project Planning, Maintaining the Task/Service Catalogue, Risk & Issue Catalogue and Maintaining the Project Cost sheets according to the policies and procedures followed within ELM.

KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES & ACTIVITIES

  • Daily Operations

    Implement the day-to-day operations assigned for the Department to ensure compliance with the established standards and procedures. Identify opportunities for continuous improvement of systems, processes and practices taking into account ‘international best practice’, improvement of business processes, cost reduction and productivity improvement. Prepare reports of Department timely and accurately to meet company and department requirements, policies and standards.

  • Analysis

    Presenting the Solution and Project Status to internal and external stakeholders, through presentations and documents. Establish the delivery Project Plan, track it and report issues to management in a timely manner Establish and maintaining the Delivery Service Catalogue and updating on a weekly basis Track the resource utilization and insure any under utilizations within the team are bought to the attention of the management Thoroughly understanding IT Service offerings and service agreements and making architectural decisions about how solutions should be designed and implemented

  • Project Execution

    Creates and executes project work plans and revises as appropriate to meet changing needs and requirements Communicates and enforces coding & documentation standards. Prioritizes multiple tasks effectively. Take ownership for ramping-up of the right resources reporting into Elm counterpart. Clear and deep understanding of all facets of the SDLC enabling governance of the technical aspects of the project. Report to Elm on the technical progress of the project ,giving early warnings on potential impact areas, risks (functional and non-functional adherence)

  • Direction of Subordinates

    Organize and supervise the activities and work of a small team of subordinates to ensure that all work within a specific area of Development activity is carried out in an efficient and procedurally compliant manner Provide formal and informal feedback to subordinates in order to ensure their continuous development.
